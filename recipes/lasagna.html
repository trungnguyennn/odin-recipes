Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef and sausage in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  3. In a large pot, boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture in the dish.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  8. Top with remaining marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
